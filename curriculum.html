Curriculum

Six phases over 24 weeks. Clear goals, short routines, steady progress.

  1. Preparation: Breath, release, posture, and awareness.
  2. Mapping: Aperture, pressure, airflow coordination.
  3. Flexibility: Slotting and partial stability.
  4. Conditioning: Progressive range & endurance.
  5. Expression: Tone, phrasing, and musicality.
  6. Transfer: Mock reps, recording, reliability.
